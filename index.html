Principles

Built for developers who need control.

Monavi packages are intentionally small. The goal is to expose clean native capability without forcing a large application structure on your project.

01 Native-first

Use platform SDKs directly where it matters.

02 Readable source

Keep behavior easy to inspect and maintain.

03 Typed APIs

Ship TypeScript surfaces for safer app integration.

04 Low ceremony

Prefer practical setup over complex abstractions.
